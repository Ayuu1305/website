About us

A 4-minute walk from Vastrapur Lake, this upscale hotel in west Ahmedabad is also 2 km from Gujarat University and 8 km from the Sabarmati Ashram temple.

Chic, modern rooms feature free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs, plus desks, minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Some have lake views, upgraded rooms add seating areas. Suites include DVD players, stereos and separate living/dining rooms. Room service is available.

Breakfast and parking are complimentary. Other amenities include a casual restaurant, a patisserie, and a Pan-Asian eatery with a terrace. There's a 24-hour gym, an outdoor pool and a spa, along with 700 sq m of event space.
